• First dropdown is to choose movement of object.
• Second dropdown is to choose what happens when the particular ball collides with another ball.
• Make Ball - creates a ball with chosen movement and interaction.
• Make Fish - creates a fish with chosen movement behavior.
• Make Switch Ball - Creates a ball whose strategy can be switched.
• Make Switch Fish - Creates a fish whose strategy can be switched.
• First dropdown is to choose from which movement of switchable object.
• Second dropdown is to choose to which movement of switchable object.
• Switch Strategy - Switches strategy of Switcher objects like switch ball and switch fish.
• Dropdown is to choose from which subset of movement to remove.
• Remove subset - Removes the necessary objects.
• Clear - Removes all objects.
• Note: * after a movement strategy means it's ball exclusive. (Will only consider non color changing parts for fish.)
